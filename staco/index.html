Dual-Layer RBAC+ Security (Role + Rank)

STACO Insurance's deployment combines functional Roles (Broker, Underwriter, Claims Adjuster) with hierarchical Ranks (L1-L5) for NDPR- and NAICOM-compliant data segregation. Encryption uses Azure TDE at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, and Azure Key Vault with automated key rotation.

Field-Level Invisibility

Sensitive financial reports, corporate policy terms, and personal customer data are dynamically masked or hidden from unauthorized users.

Settlement Approval Chains

Adjuster (L1) up to ₦1M, Unit Head (L2) up to ₦5M, Executive Committee (L3) unlimited — wired into HR ranks.

Centralized Revocation

Instant one-click global session revocation and account lockouts across all 14 modules.

Network Resilience & Offline-First PWA

To support retail field agents in regions with unreliable network coverage, the Agent Dashboard and Lead & Prospect Management modules ship as Progressive Web Apps with full offline-first operation.

IndexedDB Local Cache

Local storage for policy drafts, customer profiles, KYC artefacts, and live product rate cards — quotes generated entirely offline.

Auto Sync & Conflict Resolution

When connection is restored, the PWA reconciles deltas, replays writes, and surfaces any conflicts to the agent for resolution.

Field Productivity

Agents close sales on tablets and phones without browser loading bottlenecks — onboarding continues even with zero connectivity.

Tailored Functional Modules

14 purpose-built modules covering the entire insurance lifecycle.

Authentication & Access Control (IAM)

A high-security perimeter enforcing strict data isolation between departments via Microsoft Entra ID and dual-layer RBAC+.

Key Features

  • Microsoft Entra ID: SSO with Multi-Factor Authentication mapped to corporate credentials.
  • Dual-Layer RBAC+: Role-based access (Broker, Underwriter, Adjuster) plus Rank-based sensitivity (L1-L5).
  • Field-Level Invisibility: Sensitive financial reports and customer data masked from unauthorized users.
  • Centralized Revocation: One-click global session revocation across all modules.

Business Value

  • Total client and rate-card confidentiality protected by NDPR-compliant boundaries.
  • Eliminates unauthorized high-value claims approvals.
  • No new passwords for staff — leverages your existing Microsoft 365 directory.

Agent & Broker Dashboard

A high-speed PWA command center for field forces and broker partners to drive sales.

Key Features

  • Unified Portfolio Console: Active policies, outstanding premiums, pending renewals, and lead conversion funnels.
  • Instant Quote Generator: Direct shortcut into the Product & Quote Engine for on-the-spot onboarding.
  • Earnings Tracker: Live commissions, monthly quotas, and structured incentives.
  • Offline Mode Indicator: Synchronization status and local IndexedDB health visualizer.

Business Value

  • Field agents work from anywhere — even with poor network coverage.
  • Higher broker retention via real-time commission visibility.
  • Faster sales cycles through one-click quote generation.

Customer Management (CRM)

A structured 360-degree registry of retail customers and corporate policyholders.

Key Features

  • Single Customer View: Personal details, full policy history, claims, and billing in one record.
  • KYC Verification: Automated NIN, BVN, and Driver's License checks via verification APIs.
  • Corporate Hierarchy Engine: Group employees, assets, and group life benefits under a parent corporate account.
  • Consent & Privacy Registry: Strict NDPR auditing of data storage and communication preferences.

Business Value

Policy Management

End-to-end orchestration of the policy lifecycle from inception to expiration, renewal, or cancellation.

Key Features

  • Digital Inception Engine: Approved quotes auto-convert to active policies on payment verification.
  • Mid-Term Adjustments (MTA): Modify covered assets, sum insured, or endorsements with dynamic premium recalculation.
  • Automated Renewal Pipeline: Customized renewal offers 60, 30, and 15 days prior to expiry.
  • Lapse & Reinstatement: Grace period tracking, suspensions, and reinstatement approvals.

Business Value

  • Higher renewal rates through proactive, automated outreach.
  • Zero leakage between quote, payment, and active policy states.
  • Full audit trail of every endorsement and reinstatement.

Product & Quote Engine (Rating Core)

Standardizes risk pricing and enables instant, compliant quotation across life and non-life products.

Key Features

  • Modular Product Builder: Low-code admin interface for new products, coverage options, and deductibles.
  • Algorithmic Underwriting Rules: Premium calculation based on driver age, building location, health conditions, and other risk variables.
  • Co-Insurance & Reinsurance: Auto-splits risk and premiums across primary carriers and reinsurers per treaty.
  • Document Generator: Branded PDF quotation documents with structured benefit summaries.

Business Value

  • Eliminates underwriting leakage with consistent pricing rules.
  • Quotes generated in seconds, not days.
  • Reinsurance treaties enforced automatically — no manual splits.
