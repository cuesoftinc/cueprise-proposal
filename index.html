Tailored Functional Modules

Designed specifically for the Nigerian Oil & Gas context.

Natural Gas Logistics

A specialized module to handle the unique movement and tracking requirements of natural gas distribution.

Key Features

  • Route Optimization for delivery trucks.
  • Real-time volume tracking vs. manifest.
  • Waybill generation & digital signing.
  • Safety compliance checklists per trip.

Business Value

  • Reduce leakage/theft via digital tracking.
  • Automate invoicing upon delivery confirmation.
  • Historical data for demand forecasting.

Gas Asset Management

Lifecycle management for cylinders, storage tanks, and pumping equipment.

Functionality

  • QR/Barcode tagging for all cylinders.
  • Maintenance scheduling & alerts.
  • Depreciation calculation (Straight line/Reducing).
  • Location tracking (Warehouse vs Customer site).

  • Loan & Advance management.

Talent Management

Core ERP & Accounting
